Hawk in the morning mist

As the shadows were awakening and a scent of the morning was carried with the mist, I saw a hawk decending from the mountain top. Without a flutter of the wings it slid through the air above me and then disappeared among the trees that rimed the valley.

It left me standing there, the head in my neck, all gaze up to the grey clouds where it disappeared, hoping for it to return.

It didn't.

The hawk wasn't me.
